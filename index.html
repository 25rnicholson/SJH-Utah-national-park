Utah

Utah is home to five amazing national parks and many beautiful national monuments. Many people consider Utah to be one of the most beatiful states in the USA for obvious reasons.

National Parks

There are five National Parks in Utah that are all unique to eachother. Some consist of dense forests with teeming wildlife while others have desolate deserts with steep plateaus. There are over 2000 documented arches in Arches National Park and Zions National Park is home to many unique species of wildlife including big horn sheep, mountain lion, mule deer and many other mammals.

List of National Parks

  • Zions National Park
  • Arches National Park
  • Bryce Canyon
  • Canyon Lands
  • Capitol Reef

Utahs National Monuments
