Arches

Description

Arches is home to the largest arch span in the U.S, Arches also has the densest consentration of arches in utah. Arches was founded in 1929 by herbert hoover, then made a national park by the nixon administration.

delicate arch

delicate arch is the largest arch in Arches national park. The delicate arch is made of entrada sandstone.

Wildlife

the wildlife in arches national parks is varied with several special animals. some examples are packrats, owls, mountain lions, foxes, and skunks.
