Hovenweep National Monument

Description

Hovenweep National Monument consisits of multiple structures built from Ancestral Puebloans. Be sure to visit Hovenweep Castle and Sqaure Tower to see the ancient architecture. When the days ends, spend the night stargazing the phenominal night sky.


Things to Know

  • Hovenweep National Monument does not require a fee to enter
  • Before visiting, take caution and the dry heat and sun during the summer
  • The monument is isolated and the nearest services are 40 miles away
  • Make sure to respect the historical structures
  • Camping is $15 a night
